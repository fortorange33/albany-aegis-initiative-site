About the Albany Aegis Initiative

The Albany Aegis Initiative is a forward-looking program designed to reduce crime, combat corruption, and foster systemic progress throughout Albany, New York. By combining behavioral science, advanced technology, and economic innovation, we aim to build a resilient, accountable, and thriving city.
