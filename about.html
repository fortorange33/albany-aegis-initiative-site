A Data-Driven Approach to Public Safety

The Albany Aegis Initiative is a civic project dedicated to reducing crime, increasing accountability, and transforming governance in Albany, NY through behavioral insight, analytics, and community partnership.

Our Mission

Our multi-faceted approach combines research, technology, economic strategy, and direct community involvement to build a safer, more prosperous city for all.

The Albany Challenge: A Sobering Analysis

To solve a problem, we must first understand it. Our analysis points to a complex web of interconnected issues that stifle progress and endanger residents. A history of political corruption erodes trust, leading to stagnation and a sense of helplessness. This is compounded by economic despair and policies that, while well-intentioned, can have unintended consequences for public safety.
