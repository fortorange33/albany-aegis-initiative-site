A Smarter, Safer Albany

The Albany Aegis Initiative combines behavioral science, civic innovation, and data-driven analysis to protect and transform Albany.

View Crime Report

Our Mission

The Albany Aegis Initiative addresses crime, corruption, and economic stagnation in Albany through four strategic pillars: Behavioral Analysis, Cutting-Edge Technology, Economic Diversification, and Civic Accountability & Transparency.

Recent Impact

  • Crime fell 12% from 2023 to 2024 through targeted interventions.
  • Over 2,000 arrests logged, with expanded forensic and surveillance use.
  • AI-driven crime mapping reduced response time in high-incident areas.
  • Launch of community transparency dashboards and reporting tools.
