Interactive Crime Data Visualizations

These interactive charts explore trends in reported crimes and arrests across Albany, NY (2021–2024), offering insight into demographics, neighborhood activity, and temporal dynamics.

Shows the distribution of arrests by neighborhood. West Hill and Pine Hills top the list, highlighting spatial crime concentration.

Visualizes gender distribution among arrestees. Male arrests consistently outnumber female arrests across all years.

Compares top arrest categories by racial demographics. Highlights disparities across offense types like assault, narcotics, and theft.

Indicates which days of the week crimes are most reported. Peak activity typically occurs on Fridays and Saturdays.

Source: Albany Police Department FOIL requests (2021–2024), processed by the Albany Aegis Initiative. Visuals created with Chart.js.
