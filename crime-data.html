Key Findings

  • Crime peaked in 2023 at 9,582 incidents; dropped to 8,420 in 2024.
  • Aggravated assault and rape rates remain elevated and require targeted intervention.
  • West Hill and Pine Hills experienced the highest neighborhood-level crime rates.
  • Only 55% of 2024 cases were closed; transparency and efficiency remain priorities.
  • Average police response time for burglaries exceeded 30 minutes.
